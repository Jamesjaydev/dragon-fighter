How To Play

At the start of the game, the dragon is assigned a random amount of health.

Each weapon/spell is also assigned a random damage value each time the game is initialized. The only way to know how mich damage each weapon/spell deals is by clicking on it.

Your objective is to deal damage equal to the dragon's health. Otherwise you lose.

You're also able to choose which character you want to use and each characrer comes with a different arsenal of weapons/spells. Good luck!
